a dumb calendar

overview

  • The year is subdivided into 15 months of 24 days each.

  • Each month is subdivided into 4 weeks of 6 days each.

  • There is an extra period of 5 days (6 in leap years) at the end of the year which falls outside the regular month and weekday scheme.

  • The leap day occurs in the same year as the Gregorian leap day and is placed at the end of the year.

  • The day is subdivided into 30 hours, each of which are subdivided into 40 minutes, and each of those minutes are subdivided into exactly 72 SI seconds.

  • Epoch: Trollmas 1st, 1 AB (Anno Bachiano) = April 1, 1685 AD
