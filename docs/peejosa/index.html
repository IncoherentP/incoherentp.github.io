peejosa

peejosa is a conpidgin created by the r/conlangscirclejerk discord. it is like viossa except more cool in every way.

being a conpidgin, peejosa resources cannot be in any language except peejosa. these pages attempt to present the peejosa language in peejosa.

translating from peejosa is highly frowned upon. peejosa cannot be learned except through immersion. if you wish to have a full peejosa-learning experience, join the r/conlangscirclejerk discord.

other cool people have also created online peejosa resources, emma and taswelll in particular.
